P2P Chat Client - Manual Signalling

Instructions

  1. Caller: Click "Create Offer" to generate an SDP offer. Copy the generated text and share it with your peer via WhatsApp, phone, etc.
  2. Callee: Paste the received Offer SDP into the "Callee" section and click "Create Answer". Copy the generated Answer SDP and send it back to the caller.
  3. Caller: Paste the Answer SDP into the "Caller" section and click "Accept Answer" to establish the connection.
  4. Once connected, a "Connected: Ready to chat!" message will appear in the chat log.
  5. You can send text messages and small file attachments (e.g., images).
  6. Debug logs are printed to the browser console for troubleshooting.
